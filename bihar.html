About Bihar

Bihar, one of India's most historically significant states, is a land of rich culture, ancient history, and spiritual heritage. From the ancient university of Nalanda to the sacred city of Bodh Gaya, Bihar is a destination that blends tradition and modernity seamlessly. The state is known for its glorious past, vibrant culture, and incredible people.
